About Me

My name is James Dobbe. I am currently taking a program that teaches web development. I am pursuing a new career in computer sciences, with a strong desire to succeed. I am learning more every class than I can believe, and continue to gain confidence and drive with each lesson.

I am currently managing an Italian Restaurant, overseeing the front of house, and running the Catering department. I have learned many skills here, most all of them related to customer service, multi tasking, and time management. I think a lot of the skills I have mastered from my time on the management team at my restaurant will carry over into my developing career.

My family is most important to me. I have a beautiful wife, Becky, a 15 month old son, Cruz, and a 12 year old daughter, Morgan. We have a tabby cat, Apollo, and we have an Australian Shepard/Border Collie mix named Lucy. Some of my hobbies include playing piano and guitar, snowboarding, playing retro video games, going to Seattle Mariners games, and making pizzas.
